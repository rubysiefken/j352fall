After long, cold DMV winters, UMD students are always thrilled to spend time outdoors in the early months of spring. I took these photos as part of JOUR370, a photojournalism class I completed in spring of 2023. College Park has unique beauty in its architecture, infrastructure and natural aesthetics. While I took these photos for a grade, I am excited to capture and enjoy the natural beauty of the UMD community on my own accord this spring.
